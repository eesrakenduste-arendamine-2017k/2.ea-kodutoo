Type the words as they are shown on the screen.

The faster you type them, the more score you get.

Miss a letter and it's game over!


Instructions:

  1. Enter your name
  2. Press "New Game"
  3. Type the word on your screen. (Contains Estonian letters, make sure you can type the letters õäüö)
  4. If you do not type the word in 10 seconds, you will get no points
  5. Press ENTER when no more letters are visible
  6. A new word is shown
  7. When you press a wrong button, the game will end
  8. You will get more points the longer you play!

Have fun!

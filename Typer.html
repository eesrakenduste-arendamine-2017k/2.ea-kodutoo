Welcome To TYPER!

What is TYPER you may ask? Well to put it simply it's just a normal typing game but with Estonian words.

How the game works:

1.You start with 10 secs

2.Each word that you type in correctly gives you 1 point

3.Each word that you type in correctly adds an extra 2 seconds to your timer

4.If you make a mistake while typing, you lose 2 seconds

5.Simple as that - now go and have fun!
